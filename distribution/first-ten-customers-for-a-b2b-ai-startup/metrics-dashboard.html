Decision Rules

High impressions, low verified responses: add a sharper question next time.

Low impressions, high response quality: distribution graph is too small; comment more and send directly to better-matched people.

Low impressions, low verified responses: rewrite the hook around pain, not advice.

One comment outperforms the post: turn it into the next post within 24 hours.

Checkpoint Cadence

T+1h: impressions, comments, reposts, replies.

T+3h: strongest comment thread and post URL.

T+6h: decide whether to post a follow-up angle.

T+24h: publish standalone X post if thread slows.

T+48h: decide keep, pivot hook, or push direct outreach.
