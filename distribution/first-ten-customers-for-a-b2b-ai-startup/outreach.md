# Direct outreach

## Principle

Send the essay only when it is useful to the person receiving it.

No mass blast. No fake personalization. No scraping. No "thought you might like this" unless there is a real reason.

## Target groups

1. B2B AI founders before repeatable sales.
2. Founders running founder-led sales.
3. Investors with seed-stage AI portfolio companies.
4. Product/GTM operators helping technical founders sell.
5. Newsletter/community operators who curate AI startup playbooks.
