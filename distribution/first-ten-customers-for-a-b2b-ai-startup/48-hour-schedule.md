# 48-hour execution schedule

Use `T0` as the moment the LinkedIn anchor post goes live.

## T-60 to T-30 minutes

Goal: warm the graph before publishing.

LinkedIn:

- Search: `AI GTM`, `founder-led sales`, `first customers`, `design partners`, `B2B AI`.
- Leave 5 useful comments.
- Save the URLs of any high-signal posts in `tracker.csv`.

X:

- Search: `B2B AI startup`, `AI GTM`, `first 10 customers`, `design partners`.
- Leave 10 useful replies.
- Avoid posting the essay link.

## T-15 minutes

Goal: prepare launch assets.

- Open `social-drafts.md`.
- Copy the LinkedIn anchor post into the composer.
- Pause for final approval before publishing.
- Keep first-comment link ready.

## T0

Goal: publish the main LinkedIn asset.

- Publish LinkedIn anchor post.
- Immediately add the first comment with the canonical UTM link.
- Copy post URL into `tracker.csv` and `metrics-log.csv`.

## T+10 minutes

Goal: publish the X asset while the idea is fresh.

- Copy the X thread into X.
- Pause for final approval before publishing.
- Add the essay link as a reply after the thread.
- Copy thread URL into `tracker.csv` and `metrics-log.csv`.

## T+0 to T+60 minutes

Goal: maximize early signal.

- Reply to every meaningful LinkedIn comment.
- Reply to every meaningful X reply.
- Ask follow-up questions that invite the commenter to expand.
- Send the post to 5 warm people only if it is genuinely relevant.

Good reply shapes:

- "That is the line I keep coming back to: when does design partnership become consulting?"
- "Exactly. I think the paid part matters less for revenue and more for truth."
- "Where would you draw the line between founder-led sales and founder-led product discovery?"
- "I think the danger is outsourcing sales before the founder understands why customers buy."

## T+3 hours

Goal: add second wave without looking spammy.

- Leave 5 LinkedIn comments on relevant active posts.
- Leave 10 X replies.
- Send 5 more targeted direct notes.
- Record impressions and comments.

## T+6 hours

Goal: harvest what is working.

- Identify the best-performing sentence/comment.
- Draft one follow-up post from that angle.
- Reply to new comments.
- Record metrics.

## T+24 hours

Goal: second native post.

- Publish standalone short post 1 on X.
- If LinkedIn anchor has stalled, publish standalone short post 2 on LinkedIn.
- Send 10 direct notes to founders/operators/investors.
- Record metrics.

## T+36 hours

Goal: comment-led distribution.

- Search again for new posts on the topic.
- Leave 10 high-context comments/replies.
- If someone influential engages, deepen that thread before posting anything new.

## T+48 hours

Goal: decide next move.

If combined impressions are above 10k:

- Keep the same angle.
- Publish standalone short post 3.
- Ask 3 people who replied whether they disagree with any part.

If combined impressions are below 2k:

- Rewrite the hook around a painful founder scenario.
- Do not repost the same link.
- Use the strongest claim as a new native post.

If meaningful replies are high but impressions are low:

- Double down on comments and direct outreach.
- The idea is resonating; the distribution graph is too small.

## Metric checkpoints

Record in `metrics-log.csv`:

- T+1h
- T+3h
- T+6h
- T+24h
- T+48h
- Day 7
- Day 14
- Day 30
