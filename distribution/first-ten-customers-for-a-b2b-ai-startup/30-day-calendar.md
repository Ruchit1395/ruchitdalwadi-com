# 30-day campaign calendar

Goal: reach 100,000 total viewers/impressions across X and LinkedIn in one month without paid tools or APIs.

This calendar assumes the campaign launches on Day 0. Shift dates as needed.

## Weekly operating rule

- LinkedIn: 2-3 native posts/week plus high-quality comments.
- X: 1 thread/week, 4-6 standalone posts/week, daily replies.
- Direct outreach: 5-10 specific sends/week, only when genuinely relevant.
- Website link: usually in comments/replies, not the first line of native posts.

## Day 0 - Launch

Primary:

- Publish LinkedIn carousel or anchor post.
- Publish X thread.
- Add canonical essay link as first comment/reply.

Engagement:

- 5 LinkedIn comments before launch.
- 10 X replies before launch.
- Reply to every meaningful comment in the first hour.

Metric checkpoint:

- T+1h, T+3h, T+6h.

## Day 1 - Paid pilot angle

X:

- Post: "Free pilots are a trap for early B2B AI startups."

LinkedIn:

- If the launch post is still moving, do not publish a new LinkedIn post. Comment instead.
- If it stalled, publish a short post about paid pilots.

Direct:

- Send 5 notes to founders currently selling/piloting B2B AI.

## Day 2 - Founder attention angle

## Day 3 - Design partner angle

## Day 4 - Live workflow angle

- Search X and LinkedIn for `AI pilot`, `customer discovery`, `B2B AI GTM`.

## Day 5 - Investor-forwardable version

LinkedIn:

- Publish a shorter investor/operator version: "What I would tell a B2B AI founder before customer #1."

- Reply to investors and operators discussing AI adoption or AI SaaS.

- Send 5 notes to investors/operators who may forward it to portfolio founders.

## Day 6 - Rest + comments only

No new main post.

- 10 X replies.
- 5 LinkedIn comments.

Use this to restart the conversation without repeating the same link.

## Decision rules

Use `reach-model.md` and `reach-model.csv` for numeric pace checks.

If a post has high impressions but low replies:

- The hook travels, but the content may be too closed-ended. Add a question next time.

If a post has low impressions but high reply quality:

- The idea works. Distribution graph is too small. Comment more and send directly to better-matched people.

If a post has low impressions and low replies:

- Rewrite the opening around pain, not advice.

If one comment gets more replies than the original post:

- Turn that comment into the next post within 24 hours.

If someone influential disagrees:

- Do not defend immediately. Ask for the boundary condition: "Where does this break?"
