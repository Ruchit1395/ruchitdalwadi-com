## What not to do

- Do not publish more broad advice posts before answering Luke.
- Do not DM people with a cold link before public context is created.
- Do not publish link-first content on X or LinkedIn.
- Do not count likes/follows as target responses unless there is a real reply, comment, or forward.

## Success threshold

The rescue sprint is working if it creates any of:

- 1 more target-audience reply,
- 1 repost/share by a founder/operator/investor,
- 100+ incremental X views,
- 250+ incremental LinkedIn impressions,
- or a direct response that can be logged in `reply-source-scorecard.csv`.
