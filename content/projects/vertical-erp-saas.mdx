---
title: Workflow software replacing spreadsheets for a specific operator
domain: ERP SaaS
pillar: Product
problem: An industry where operators still ran the business on a stitched-together spreadsheet stack. Generic ERPs didn't fit the workflow; building a vertical one meant going deep on the operator's actual day.
role: Product on workflow, onboarding, and the data-migration motion.
insight: For vertical SaaS, the onboarding *is* the product. A customer who can't get their existing data into the new system doesn't churn — they never start. We built the migration as a concierge service before we built half the features.
signal: B2B vertical SaaS.
status: Shipped
updates:
  - date: 2022-04-01
    note: Replaced self-serve onboarding with a one-day data-migration concierge. Time-to-first-value dropped from weeks to a day.
  - date: 2023-02-10
    note: Spreadsheets-to-system migration tool generalized; activation rate roughly doubled.
---

The big lesson: in vertical SaaS, the data migration is where the deal is won or lost. Anonymized; the playbook generalizes.
