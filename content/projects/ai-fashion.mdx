domain: AI × Fashion

problem: Helping shoppers find pieces they'd actually buy in a category where taste is the product. Recommendation, shoppable feeds, and AI-powered styling — but in a way the user trusted instead of resented.
role: Product + AI scoping. Worked on the recommendation stack and the discovery UX.
insight: In taste-driven categories, the recommendation engine has to be *visibly considerate* — not just accurate. Users forgive a wrong suggestion if the system shows it understood why they might like it. Black-box "for you" feeds train distrust.
signal: Consumer scale; visual-first.
