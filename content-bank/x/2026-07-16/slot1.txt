Your scariest production failure will not throw an error.

It will look like this: responses drift 15 percent shorter over a month. Hedging creeps up. An integration starts returning empty strings and the model, being helpful, works around the silence so gracefully that every individual output still reads fine.

Loud failures get fixed the same day because they page someone. Silent ones compound for weeks because no single output is wrong enough to report, and you cannot see a distribution shift by scrolling examples.

The unlock: you do not need to define correct to detect different. Sample 1 percent of traffic, track a few weekly averages, alert on movement. Correctness is hard to measure. Change is easy, and change is almost always the earlier signal.
