Timeline of an LLM incident, reconstructed at the postmortem:

Day 1: a partner API starts returning empty strings. No error. Just silence.
Days 2 to 18: the model improvises around the gap. Beautifully. Confidently. Daily.
Day 12: a customer replies "interesting numbers." Nobody hears the italics.
Day 19: a human says the output feels a bit off. The vibe is escalated.
Day 20: someone finally greps the logs and finds 19 days of graceful improvisation.

Retro action item, in effect: our most reliable monitoring system is currently a feeling. The actual fix was one alert on empty retrievals. The feeling got a raise.
