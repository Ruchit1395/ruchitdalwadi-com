The rejected-output audit. Next time your team throws away an AI draft, don't reach for a better prompt. Run four questions on the corpse:

1. What context was missing? The model can't cite a policy it never saw.

2. Which decision did we hand over too early? Judgment calls stay human until the model earns them.

3. What would a reviewer check in 30 seconds? That check becomes your acceptance test.

4. What should the model never change without asking? That becomes a hard constraint in the system.

Answer those and you've written most of a workflow spec: inputs, context, constraints, checks.

A prompt library goes stale in a month. A spec built from your own failures compounds.
