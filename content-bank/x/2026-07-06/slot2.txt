Your team just rejected an AI draft. Do not reach for a better prompt yet. Interrogate the corpse.

What context was missing? The model cannot cite a policy it never saw.

Which decision did we hand over too early? Judgment calls stay human until the model earns them.

What would a reviewer check in 30 seconds? That check is your acceptance test.

What should the model never change without asking? That is a hard constraint.

Answer those four and you have written most of a workflow spec: inputs, context, constraints, checks. A prompt library goes stale in a month. A spec built from your own failures compounds.
