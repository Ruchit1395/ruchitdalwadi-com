Take the last AI output your team rejected and finish this sentence: "It failed because it did not know ___."

Not "because the prompt was weak." The blank has to name something the model could not have known: the current policy, the approved format, the fact that this customer already churned once and came back on a discount.

Then fix the blank permanently. Attach the doc, add the example, write the constraint into the system prompt. One blank, one fix, applied to every future run.

Rewording the prompt treats the symptom. The blank is the disease, and it is usually curable with one attachment.
