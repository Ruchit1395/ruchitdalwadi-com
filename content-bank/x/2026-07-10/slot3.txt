Your agent's stop condition is a claim until you sabotage it.

Add one line to the instructions: "If a required input is missing or an action is irreversible, stop and report what you have." Then delete a required field from a test task on purpose and watch.

If it halts and reports, you have an agent. If it invents a value and keeps rolling, you just found this week's real work, and a drill is a much cheaper place to find it than a customer's shipment.

Rerun the same sabotage after every major prompt or model change. Stopping behavior regresses quietly, and no dashboard is going to tell you when it does.
