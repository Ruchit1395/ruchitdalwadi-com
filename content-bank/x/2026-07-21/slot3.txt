Anthropic is offering grants up to $50,000 in Claude usage credits to researchers. The goal is to accelerate cures for rare diseases. This is their first focused call within the 'AI for Science' program.

For founders and PMs, this is a signal: AI for science is heating up. If you're building in biotech, pharma, or medical research, look for these targeted programs. It's not just about general-purpose models; it's about applying them to specific, high-impact domains where the data is rich and the need is urgent. This kind of grant can de-risk early-stage research and open doors to new partnerships. Pay attention to where the big players are allocating compute.
