Stages of an unscoped AI coding session:

1. Hope. The task is described in nine words. What could go wrong.
2. The diff arrives. 40 files. You were expecting 4.
3. Bargaining. "Maybe I will just review the important files." There are 40 important files.
4. Depression. The tool asks if you would like it to also update the README. It already has.
5. Acceptance. git reset --hard. The commit history never has to know.

I keep a 5-line brief taped to my monitor now. Grief is optional. Scoping is not.
