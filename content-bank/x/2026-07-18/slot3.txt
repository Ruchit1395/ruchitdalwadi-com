The quickest demo-vs-production test costs one email.

Take whatever impressed you this week and feed it the ugliest real input from last week: the forwarded chain with the actual question buried in reply seven, the half-filled form, the message that is 40 percent typos.

Demos are built on curated inputs, so this one artifact shows you what the polish was hiding. If it handles the mess, you might have a product. If the input needs cleaning first, you have a demo plus an unpaid preprocessing job, and you already know who the preprocessor is.

Production does not send clean inputs. It sends that email chain, hourly.
