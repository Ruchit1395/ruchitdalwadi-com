Building and shipping iOS apps? A small but mighty shift in workflow just landed that can smooth out your development and security process.

Claude Code on desktop now works directly with the iOS simulator. This means you can build and run your app, and the simulator pops up in a panel right next to your code. No more alt-tabbing or juggling windows. It's a simple change, but it removes friction from the tight feedback loop of app development.

Even more impactful: the Claude Security plugin is now in beta for Claude Code. It scans your changes for vulnerabilities before you commit them. Integrating security checks this early in the cycle can prevent a lot of headaches later. Think of the regressions avoided, the tickets deflected, and the overall trust built into your product when security is baked in from the first commit. This is how you ship faster, and safer.
